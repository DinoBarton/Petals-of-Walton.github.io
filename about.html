PLEASE CALL 01932 222016 FOR ALL BESPOKE BOUQUETS AND FUNERAL TRIBUTES

Wonderful bouquets, displays and tributes for any occasion

If you're looking for absolutely top quality florists delivering wonderful bouquets, displays and tributes for any occasion in Surrey and the surrounding area then you're in luck - because that's exactly what we're renowned for.

We have a customer loyalty card in operation such that if you have 6 seperate purchases and you get your card stamped this means that on the 7th visit you receive 50% off of your next purchase.

If you would rather talk to a real person, all you need to do is pick up the phone, call our telephone number - 01932 222016 - and tell us your requirements. We'll do the rest.Or if you've got a few minutes to spare and you're somewhere near Walton-on-Thames, come and have a good look around our shop. There are always plenty of ideas for gifts as well as a fantastic selection of fresh flowers, pot plants and specially selected shrubs.
